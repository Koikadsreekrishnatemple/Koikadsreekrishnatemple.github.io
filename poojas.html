vazhipadukal

The main vazhipadu of our temple is "NAZHIYUM PIDI".And other vazhipadu are

  • Pushpanjali
  • Kadumpayasam
  • Kuttupayasam
  • Kalpayasam

Festivals

Sri koikad sreekrishna Swami Temple , the uthsavam is conducting at the birthday of our god on rohini(star) of Kumbha, is very auspicious to the Hindus.The utsavam is on two day festival, on that days various poojas,vazhipadu and various programmes are conducting.And other festivals are niraputhari,mandalamasa poojas and sreekrishnajayandhi etc....Ashtamirohini is the birth time of Sree krishna. It is celebrated with great rejoicing and various cultural programmes to commemorate the incarnation of Lord Krishna at Ashtamirohini in the month of Chingam
